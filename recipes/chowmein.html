Description

Chow Mein is a popular Chinese stir-fried noodle dish loaded with crunchy vegetables, tender protein (like chicken, shrimp, or tofu), and savory sauces. It’s quick, flavorful, and customizable—perfect for a delicious weeknight meal!

Ingredients

  • 200g chow mein noodles
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1-inch ginger
  • 1 cup protein
  • 2 cups mixed veggies
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 tsp vinegar
  • ½ tsp sugar
  • Salt & pepper
  • Green onions

Steps

  1. Cook noodles as per package instructions, drain, and toss with a little oil to prevent sticking.
  2. Heat oil in a wok/pan, sauté garlic and ginger until fragrant.
  3. Add protein and stir-fry until cooked. Remove and set aside.
  4. Stir-fry veggies on high heat until crisp-tender (2-3 mins).
  5. Add noodles, protein, soy sauce, oyster sauce, vinegar, sugar, salt, and pepper. Toss well.
  6. Garnish with green onions and serve hot!
