Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, rich meat sauce, creamy ricotta, and melted cheese. Baked until golden and bubbly, it's a comforting, hearty meal perfect for family dinners or gatherings. Simple to prepare yet always satisfying, lasagna is a crowd-pleasing favorite.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Marinara sauce
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Grated Parmesan cheese
  • Garlic

Steps

  1. Cook meat with garlic, onion, and seasoning; mix in marinara sauce.
