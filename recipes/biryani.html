Biryani

Home

Description

Biryani is a fragrant, spiced rice dish layered with tender meat (chicken, mutton, or beef), aromatic basmati rice, and a blend of herbs and saffron. Slow-cooked to perfection, it’s a rich, flavorful feast that’s a staple in South Asian cuisine.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 4 cups water
  • 1 bay leaf, 2-3 cardamom pods, 1 cinnamon stick, 4-5 cloves
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 500g chicken/mutton/beef
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp turmeric, 1 tsp red chili powder, 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice, salt to taste
  • 2 tomatoes,

Steps

  1. Mix meat with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, spices, lemon juice, and salt and let it marinate for at least 1 hour
  2. Boil soaked rice with whole spices and salt until 70% cooked. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a heavy pot, layer: ghee → fried onions → marinated meat → mint/coriander → tomatoes → rice.
  4. Repeat layers, drizzle saffron milk on top.
  5. Cover with a tight lid and cook on low heat (dum) for 25-30 mins
  6. Let it rest for 10 mins, then fluff gently and serve with raita or salad.
